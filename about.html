Who We Are

About Us

A community organization dedicated to enhancing the vibrancy and vitality of Benicia, California — working across four key areas to make our city a better place to live, work, and visit.

Our Mission

Enhance the vibrancy and vitality of our community through dedicated efforts in visual beautification, robust support for local businesses, heartfelt acknowledgement of our military members, and the promotion of tourism.

What We Do

01

Beautification

We take pride in Benicia's appearance, organizing efforts to improve public spaces, streetscapes, and neighborhoods throughout the city.

02

Local Business Support

We celebrate and champion the local businesses that give Benicia its unique character, helping them thrive and grow.

03

Military Recognition

Through our Military Banner Program, we honor the men and women who have served our country by displaying their portraits on Benicia's streets.

04

Tourism Promotion

We share the beauty and history of Benicia with visitors, helping showcase everything our city has to offer.
